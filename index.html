INSTRUCTIONS
X

1. Choose fluid from the dropdown menu

2. If the fluid is different than water - input the percent of the fluid

3. Fill in all fields from the section “Input Data”
* You can view more info about them in the section “Legends”

4. Click the button to start the calculation

5. You can see the results in the section “Output Data”

6. You can view more info in the section “Diagram”

LEGEND
X

Vsys - water quantity in the system

tS - submission temperature

tF - filling temperature

Hst - static height

Vcalc - minimal required vessel volume

Vn - nominal vessel volume

pst - static pressure

p0 - minimum working pressure

pini - initial pressure

pfil - pressure for filling

pfin - final pressure

ppzh - pressure reducer pressure

psv - safety valve pressure
